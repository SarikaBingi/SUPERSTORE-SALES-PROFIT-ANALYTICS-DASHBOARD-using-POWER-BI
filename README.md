# 📊 Superstore Business Intelligence Dashboard

A Power BI analytics project with 5 interactive dashboards analyzing sales, profit, products, and customers for a retail business (2014–2017).

## 🧭 Project Overview
Built using Superstore-style transaction data, this project breaks down business performance by Category, Segment, and Region, giving stakeholders a clear view of sales trends, profitability, product performance, and customer value.

## 📈 Dashboards & Key Insights

- **Executive Dashboard** — High-level KPIs (Total Sales ~$2.3M, Profit $286K, Orders 5K). Strong sales growth 2015–2017; Home Office is the most profitable segment.
- **Sales Analytics** — Technology leads category sales (36.4%); West/East regions outperform others; sales up ~47% YoY.
- **Product Analytics** — 2K products across 3 categories; Technology drives highest sales despite fewer listings; discounts rise sharply for Office Supplies and Technology.
- **Customer Analytics** — 793 customers; Consumer segment contributes most profit (46.8%); a small group of top customers drive disproportionate revenue.
- **Profit Analytics** — Total profit $286.4K; Office Supplies has the best margins; ~20% of orders are unprofitable; California/New York lead in profit by state.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
Power BI Desktop, Power Query, DAX, Excel/CSV
